What is Financial Ratio....

 

A Financial Ratio is a relative ration of two selected numerical values taken form organization's financial reports.
These numerical values taken mainly from the Balance Sheet and the Income Statement.
Financial ratios are categorized according to the financial aspects of an organization.
  Liquidity Ratio
  Profitability Ratio
  Turnover Ratio
  Solvency Ratio
